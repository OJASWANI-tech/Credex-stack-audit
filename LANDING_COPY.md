## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ Matrix)

### Q1: Do I need to sync my corporate bank cards or link platform administrative accounts?
**A1:** Absolutely not. StackAudit is engineered to be entirely non-invasive and privacy-first. There are no data connections, API syncs, or account registrations required. You simply input your current seat numbers and tier selections manually, and our client-side engine calculates optimization insights instantly.

### Q2: How does the calculation engine determine my optimization savings?
**A2:** The tool cross-evaluates your designated global Team Size against the seat quantities and plan tiers selected for individual applications. It identifies pricing discrepancies—such as seat surpluses, redundant plan levels, or over-provisioned enterprise packages—and contrasts them against optimized pricing matrices.

### Q3: Where does my subscription and team data go after running an audit?
**A3:** Your data never leaves your machine. The calculation logic executes entirely within your browser context using local state compilation. No database records are written, ensuring 100% security compliance with zero data retention leakage risks.

### Q4: What happens when I click the "Join List" action button?
**A4:** Joining our notification list adds your email to our priority update pipeline. We will notify you the moment wholesale pricing pools open up or when deep architectural audits expand to support complex cloud infrastructure providers.

### Q5: How frequently is the underlying vendor pricing tier data refreshed?
**A5:** Our system pricing data matrix (`PRICING_DATA.md`) is verified and updated at regular intervals to align with public rate adjustments across Tier-1 AI software providers including Cursor, OpenAI, and Anthropic.
