# ECONOMICS.md: Unit Economics & Growth Modeling

## 1. Value of a Converted Lead to Credex
A converted lead is defined as an enterprise account that bridges from the StackAudit diagnostic funnel to book a custom optimization consultation with Credex, eventually purchasing wholesale credits.

* **Assumed Credex Core Monetization Model:** Taking a 20% performance fee on verified annualized software savings achieved through wholesale procurement pooling.
* **Average Client Profile:** Series A tech team with a baseline SaaS developer tool run-rate of $50,000/year. 
* **Average Optimization Yield:** 20% reduction in waste ($10,000 in saved capital).
* **Performance Fee Realized:** $10,000 * 20% = $2,000 per optimized client.
* **Estimated Lifetime Value (LTV):** Assuming an average client retention period of 3 years through recurring annual optimization renewals.
* **Lead Value Calculation:** 
  $$\text{LTV} = \$2,000 \times 3 = \$6,000$$

## 2. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by Channel
Because our GTM strategy relies exclusively on targeted zero-dollar outbound and niche professional community placement, paid media spend is $0. CAC is calculated purely by valuing engineering/founder outbound time at an internal cost rate of $50/hour.

| Channel | Time Invested per Batch | Outputs Generated | Estimated CAC |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **LinkedIn Outbound** | 5 Hours (Sourcing & messaging 150 VPs) | 30 Conversions | **$8.33** ($250 / 30) |
| **Rands Leadership Slack** | 2 Hours (Drafting high-signal breakdown) | 30 Conversions | **$3.33** ($100 / 30) |
| **Hacker News Launch** | 3 Hours (Formatting launch text + tracking thread) | 40 Conversions | **$3.75** ($150 / 40) |

* **Weighted Average CAC Matrix:** ~$5.00 per acquired user running an audit.

## 3. Funnel Conversion Rates & Profitability Threshold
To maintain clear operational profitability, our customer lifetime revenue value must confidently exceed our combined acquisition friction costs ($\text{LTV} > \text{CAC}$). 

### The Conversion Funnel Target Map
1. **Audit Completed:** 1,000 unique teams (Total CAC = $5,000)
2. **Consultation Booked (10% Conversion Rate):** 100 discovery calls scheduled.
3. **Credit Purchase / Deal Closed (20% Win Rate):** 20 finalized enterprise optimization accounts.

### Funnel Math Proof
* **Total Gross Revenue Generated:** $20 \text{ closed accounts} \times \$6,000 \text{ LTV} = \$120,000$.
* **Total Funnel Acquisition Cost:** $1,000 \text{ audits} \times \$5 \text{ CAC} = \$5,000$.
* **Net Unit Profit Matrix:** $\$120,000 - \$5,000 = \$115,000$.
* **Minimum Viable Profitability Frontier:** The baseline tool achieves profitability the exact moment the collective funnel conversion rate from **Audit Completed $\rightarrow$ Credit Purchase scales above 0.08%**.

## 4. The $1M ARR 18-Month Scaling Roadmap
For StackAudit to act as a foundational acquisition machine driving $1,000,000 in Annual Recurring Revenue within an 18-month timeline, the following operational benchmarks must be sustained:

* **Target Active Customer Run Rate:** At a net revenue profile of $2,000/year per client, Credex requires **500 active paying enterprises** under optimization management.
* **Total Lead Flow Requirements:** Working backwards from our 2% overall funnel conversion velocity ($1,000 \text{ audits} \rightarrow 20 \text{ closed deals}$), the platform must capture and process **25,000 completed browser audits** within 18 months.
* **Monthly Audit Velocity:** ~1,388 unique audits processed globally per month.
* **Core Requirements for Execution:** 
  1. The software optimization heuristics must expand to dynamically cover enterprise tiers of cloud platforms (AWS/GCP infrastructure spend pools).
  2. Implement programmatic sharing hooks—forcing the calculator to generate an exportable `.md` summary patch that VPs can paste directly into internal engineering Slack channels, triggering viral organic peer-to-peer distribution.
